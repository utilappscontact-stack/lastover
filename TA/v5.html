Why tiger and goats?
Tiger Trap is inspired by classic South Asian hunt games such as Bagh Chal and Aadu Puli Aatam. One tiger is powerful alone. The goats only win by closing lanes together.
That asymmetry is the heart of this game. It turns an ancient hunt into a fast modern puzzle: short levels, readable moves, abstract visuals, and no heavy art dependency.
